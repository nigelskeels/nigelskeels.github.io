Play mode Note On One shot

slices

    Instructions
  1. The number buttons select a slice to play (You can change the amount of slices at the bottom, and the playback mode)
  2. If you have a mic plugged in, allow access, then press the record button, sample something then press stop.
  3. Now your sample is sliced across the number buttons.
  4. Whatever slice button you pressed last gets assigned to the keys below, to play at different pitches
  5. You can plug a midi keyboard in to play too
